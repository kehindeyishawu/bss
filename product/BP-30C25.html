Product Overview

Free to Fit.
Perfect for
Your Business.

The Sharp BP-30C25 is Sharp's premier compact A3 colour MFP — delivering vivid full-colour output at 25 pages per minute, backed by the most powerful specifications in its class. With 5 GB of shared memory, a 128 GB SSD (upgradeable), and a first colour copy time of 11.0 seconds, it is built to handle the most demanding colour document workflows with confidence.

Scanning throughput reaches 52 originals per minute with the 100-sheet RSPF, and an expansive file format library — including PDF/A, searchable PDF, OOXML, XPS, compact PDF, and encrypted PDF — ensures every scanned document is immediately usable in any downstream system. Print resolution reaches an equivalent 9,600 × 600 dpi for exceptional colour and detail reproduction.

Optional fax (MX-FX15), optional Adobe PostScript 3 (MX-PK13), and optional wireless LAN (MX-EB18) round out a feature set that makes the BP-30C25 one of the most complete compact colour MFPs available. It supports A3W (12" × 18") media and paper weights up to 256 g/m² — covering every print application from everyday documents to heavyweight marketing materials.

What It Does

Key Features

🎨
Full Colour at 25 ppm

Produce vibrant, professional-quality colour output at 25 pages per minute — and mono at the same speed — making full-colour printing a practical, everyday part of your workflow.

💾
5 GB Memory + 128 GB SSD

With the highest memory specification in its class, the BP-30C25 handles complex colour jobs, large document queues, and extensive filing with ease. Upgradeable SSD expands filing capacity further.

🖨️
9,600 × 600 dpi Equivalent Resolution

Sharp's advanced colour print engine delivers output at an equivalent 9,600 × 600 dpi — producing remarkably smooth gradients, precise colour reproduction, and crisp fine detail.

⚡
52 opm Scanning Throughput

The 100-sheet RSPF scans at up to 52 originals per minute — the fastest in the BP-30C series — ensuring high-volume colour scanning is never a bottleneck in your workflow.

📁
Comprehensive Document Filing

Store up to 5,000 pages in standard filing across main, custom, and quick file folders. With optional SSD upgrade, capacity grows significantly for high-volume archiving environments.

📠
Optional Fax & PostScript 3

Optional MX-FX15 fax module adds full Super G3/G3 capability. Optional MX-PK13 adds Adobe PostScript 3 for advanced graphic arts and design printing workflows.
