Product Overview

Premium Colour
for the
BP-30C25.

The Sharp BP-GT30YA/MA/CA is the genuine OEM high-yield CMY (Cyan, Magenta, Yellow) colour toner set for the Sharp BP-30C25 A3 colour multifunction printer. Each cartridge delivers approximately 15,000 pages at 5% coverage — a 50% higher yield than the equivalent BP-22C25 colour toners — making it the right choice for high-volume colour printing environments.

The three cartridges in this set cover the full colour spectrum. Combined with the BP-GT30BA black toner, they provide the complete CMYK consumables solution for the BP-30C25. As genuine Sharp consumables, each cartridge is precisely formulated for the BP-30C25's advanced colour laser engine — ensuring accurate colour reproduction, smooth gradients, rich saturation, and consistent quality across the full yield.

Each colour — BP-GT30YA (Yellow), BP-GT30MA (Magenta), and BP-GT30CA (Cyan) — is available individually, allowing you to replace only the depleted colour without discarding remaining toner in other cartridges. This flexibility makes genuine Sharp toner not just the quality choice, but the economical one for any serious colour print operation.
